Project Description

Usability, Presentation, and Functionalities

The project is to redesign the COMP 1950 website. However, we are not redesigning without reasons. While giving COMP 1950 a new look can create a fresh new experience for its visitors, that is not a good enough reason. Instead, the primary goal here is to fulfill the needs to improve the website's ease of use, presentation of content, and common functionalities. In addition to restyling the website with a great looking new theme, our team has focused on transforming the website into something that is intuitively simple and easy to use.

Design Rationale

Clarity and Simplicity

We believe the most important content in the COMP 1950 website is text. Specifically, the lecture notes. Therefore, we've considered many choices involving typography, and you will see that our choices reflect the idea of making texts easy and pleasant to read. We achieved that by using a beautiful font, larger font sizes, and a neutral and soothing colour palette. We are also aware that students enjoy minimizing the time they need to spend on reviewing online content, so providing a faster searching and browsing experience was paramount. We were able to achieve half of this goal earlier by taking care of the font, because reading is now easy and fast. We then found out that scrolling and navigating through the enormous amount of content wasn't as fast and as easy as it could be, so we added features such as, a static navigation menu that stays on top of your screen, an automatic hiding search bar attached underneath the navigation menu, and automatic scrolling links that take you to any points of interest on the current page. As a result, we've successfully reduced the amount of time spent scrolling, searching, and squinting eyes when using the new COMP 1950 website. That's clarity and simplicity.
